How it works

Up and running
in under 3 minutes.

01
Scan your receipt
Take a photo of any grocery receipt. We read it, match every item to a real product, and add it to your pantry — dated and counted. On the first scan you confirm a couple of things; after that it's instant.
02
Get the nudge before it turns
One notification, the evening before something expires. Not a barrage — just the single alert that actually saves the spinach and prompts you to use the cheddar.
03
Shop without guessing
Your shopping list knows what's already in your pantry. Items you have show up dimmed with a "have it" tag. You leave the store with what you need — not what you already have.

Why we're building this

We got tired of
throwing food away too.
